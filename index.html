Gem Gamble
A test of wallets and wits.

Gem Gamble is a game of math and memory.

You are given a random bling budget to spend on gems.
The objective is to spend all of the money in you bling budget.
Not a dime more, not a dime less.

Below is a selection of gems you can buy.
Each gem has a randomly assigned price that is hidden.

To reveal the price of the gem, click on it.
The price will be shown in your wallet.

As you continue to click on gems, the price of your basket will increase.

To win, use all of the money to fill up your basket, but remember, going over budget will result in buyers remorse!
